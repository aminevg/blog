I'm Amine, an engineer splitting my time between Paris and Tokyo. I work
at the boundary between infrastructure and developer productivity. The
companies I pick tend to be building platforms for other engineers.

My day job is at [3-Shake](https://3-shake.com), a Japanese SRE consultancy.
Before that, Datadog. Before that, smaller product teams — the kind where
you learn to run systems by breaking them first.

Away from work, I read, run long distances, and wrestle with how Japanese
fits together. This blog is where the notes that don't fit anywhere else
end up.
