I'm Amine, an engineer splitting my time between Paris and Tokyo. I work on
the boundary between infrastructure and developer productivity, mostly at
companies building platforms for other engineers.

My day job is at [3-Shake](https://3-shake.com), a Japanese SRE consultancy.
Before that I was at Datadog, and before that at a series of smaller product
teams where I learned — mostly by breaking things — how to run systems in
production.

Outside of work I spend time reading, running long distances badly, and
attempting to understand how Japanese actually fits together. This blog is
where I put the notes that don't fit anywhere else.
