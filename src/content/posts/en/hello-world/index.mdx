Welcome to the first post on this blog. This is a long-form space for notes on
software, SRE, and the occasional tangent about life between Paris and Tokyo.

## Why another blog

Most of my writing for the past few years has lived in internal docs, Notion
pages, and Slack threads. The goal here is simpler: **publish things that
future-me would be glad to find**, and occasionally useful to someone else.

<Callout variant="note">
  Posts are typically drafted in both English and Japanese, but not everything
  will be translated — some thoughts only make sense in one language.
</Callout>

## What to expect

- Hands-on notes from building, debugging, and operating systems at scale.
- Short reflections on tooling choices, from Terraform modules to editor
  bindings.
- The occasional longer piece on career, learning habits, or team dynamics.

Thanks for stopping by.
