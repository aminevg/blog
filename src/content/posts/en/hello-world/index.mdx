Welcome to the first post on this blog. This is a long-form space for notes
on software and SRE. Plus the occasional tangent about life between Paris
and Tokyo.

## Why another blog

My writing these past years has lived in internal docs, Notion pages, and
Slack threads. The goal here is simpler: **publish notes that future-me
would be glad to find**. Sometimes they'll be useful to someone else too.

<Callout variant="note">
  I draft posts in both English and Japanese. Not every post gets translated —
  some thoughts belong in one language.
</Callout>

## What to expect

- Hands-on notes from building, debugging, and operating systems at scale.
- Short reflections on tooling choices, from Terraform modules to editor
  bindings.
- The occasional longer piece on career, learning habits, or team dynamics.

Thanks for stopping by.
